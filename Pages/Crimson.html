The Boards: The boards of The Crimson, in no particular order, are: News, Editorial, Arts, Sports, Magazine (Fifteen Minutes, or “FM”), Blog (Flyby), Business, Technology, Design, and Multimedia.

The Beginning: The Harvard Crimson, the nation’s oldest continuously published daily college newspaper, was founded in 1873.

The Building:

The Point: The Harvard Crimson is the only breakfast-table daily newspaper in Cambridge, Mass. The Crimson publishes every morning, Monday through Friday, except on federal and University holidays.

The War: We have been in war with the lampoon since 1901, also known as the semi-secret Sorrento Square social organization that used to occasionally publish a so-called humor magazine.
